Importance Of Trademark Registration in India :-

  1. * Legal protection against fake and fraudulent products is provided by this registration.
  2. * A customer is able to identify a product due to its name or logo.
  3. * An intangible asset is created for the owner which can be transferred, franchised, or sold.
  4. Customers are loyal to the products because of the goodwill and trust created by the trademark.
  5. A trademark is a asset. It remains with the firm forever.
  6. Trademark helps a start-up business to build brand trust, no matter which field the business is expanding in

Documentation Required for Trade Mark Registration

Protecting trademarks under the Trademark Act of 1999 is a mandatory thing in India. Few documents are required to be submitted while registering a trademark. Logo design or numbers or trademark. Description of the applicant including address, name, nature of the business, etc. The service or the product which is required for the registration. Power of the Attorney (Signed by the Applicant). The date of the first usage of the trademark. One should carry a list of all the goods and services (provided by the firm or the business or the person) at the time of the registration.
