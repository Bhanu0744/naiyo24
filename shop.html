Shop & Establishment Registration

Home > CA Services > Shop & Establishment Registration

Registering your business under the Shops and Establishments Act is a critical step that must be completed within 30 days of establishment. This registration serves as the official documentation for conducting commercial operations and is considered the primary license requirement. It's important to note that the Shops and Establishments Act is governed by state-specific regulations and laws in India. Failing to obtain the required registration can result in severe consequences, including imprisonment and substantial financial fines. Therefore, complying with this legal requirement is essential to avoid potential legal liabilities and ensure the smooth operation of your business.
